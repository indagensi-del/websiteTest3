What holds us together

Three Pillars of Faith

Everything we do flows from these. They shape how we gather, how we grow, and how we give ourselves away.

Worship

We lift our voices together, lingering in His presence. Worship isn't a warm-up — it's the heartbeat of everything.

Community

Faith was never meant to be carried alone. We do life in small groups, around dinner tables, and through the hard seasons.

Outreach

Grace given is grace meant to overflow. We serve our city, feed the hungry, and carry hope beyond these walls.
